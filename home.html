Welcome City Hospital Multan

Since Its opening, City Hospital. has been expanding Its services and equipment to meet the growing healthcare needs of the human community. The objectives of City Hospital are to provide tertiary level patient care and serve as a referral hospital also to conduct teaching/ training of doctors and other health workers at various levels in the field of medicine and surgery.

We Value Humanity

We concern about values that preserve humanity in this diverse setting by expert staff and trained staff members. These expert and trained heads know certain valuable approaches like respect for others, commitment to integrity and ethical practice, commitment to justice and excellence, compassion, and safe relationship-centered care.
